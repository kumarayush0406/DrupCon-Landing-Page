About
DrupalCon

DrupalCon is a premiere conference to connect with one of the largest opensource community, and learn about the future of web technologies happening on the Drupal platform.

DrupalCon is an experience filled with trainings, sessions, keynotes, and a full day dedicated to contributing in person. The community also offers training and industry summits; multiple days of sessions, where everyone is invited to talk, learn and explore about Drupal; along with a dedicated contribution day.
